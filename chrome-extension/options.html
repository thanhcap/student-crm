Relationship CRM Capture

Paste the capture token and endpoint from your CRM’s Settings → Capture page. They are stored only in this browser and sent only to your own CRM.

  1. In the CRM, open Settings → Capture and generate a token.
  2. Copy both the token and the endpoint and paste them above.
  3. Visit any linkedin.com/in/… profile — a “+ Add to CRM” button appears bottom-right.

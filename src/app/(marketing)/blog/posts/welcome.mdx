# Why we built Relationship CRM

Most CRMs are built for enterprise sales teams with 50-person pipelines and quarterly reviews.
We built this one for the rest of us — students, freelancers, consultants — who network by
actually talking to people, not by filling out Salesforce fields.

The idea is simple: your relationships are the most valuable asset you have, and they die from
neglect, not from lack of care. Software should carry the remembering — who you talked to,
what you promised, when to follow up — so you can carry the conversation.
